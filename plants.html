Monstera Deliciosa

Swiss Cheese Plant

(128 reviews)
$29.99 $34.99

The Monstera Deliciosa, also known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, is a tropical favorite with its large, glossy leaves that develop unique splits and holes as they mature. This easy-care plant adds a dramatic tropical feel to any space and is perfect for both beginners and experienced plant parents.

Light

Bright, indirect light

Water

When top 2" of soil is dry

Pet Friendly

Toxic to pets

Size

Can grow 6-8 feet tall

1

Care Instructions

  • Water when the top 2 inches of soil feel dry
  • Prefers bright, indirect light but can tolerate medium light
  • Wipe leaves with a damp cloth to remove dust
  • Fertilize monthly during growing season (spring-summer)
  • Rotate plant periodically for even growth
